The Studio

Bespoke Interfusion of Craftsmanship and Luxury

Located in the heart of Pune's design hub, Urban Frill is an elite architectural furnishing studio. We engineer complete soft-furnishing transformations for premium residences, commercial landscapes, corporate builders, and luxury hotel projects across Maharashtra.

In close alignment with prominent architects and interior designers, we curate custom window treatments, wall-coverings, and high-performance textiles. Our core philosophy bridges aesthetic sophistication with durable structural layout engineering.

  • Uncompromising Quality: Globally sourced textiles and components tested for ultimate longevity.
  • Tailored Customization: Tailoring products down to exact millimeter specifications for flawless integration.
  • Professional Consultation: Structured spatial site evaluation, texture pairing, and functional advice.
  • Premium Global Brands: Direct gallery access to world-class elite catalogs and international textile labels.
  • Expert Precision Installation: Dedicated in-house tech teams guaranteeing clean execution.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom curtains and blinds?

Yes. Urban Frill designs, stitches, and installs custom curtains and blinds, including motorized tracks, blackout drapery, roller blinds, and Roman blinds for homes, offices, and hospitality projects.

Do you provide on-site measurement and consultation?

Yes. Our design consultants can visit your site for precise measurements, material sampling, and layout advice before you commit to an order. Call or WhatsApp us to schedule a visit.

What areas do you serve?

We serve residential and commercial clients across India from our studio in Pune, and can coordinate remote consultations and shipping for projects further afield.

Can you reupholster an existing sofa instead of replacing it?

Yes. We restore and reupholster existing sofas using Martindale-abrasion-certified, stain-resistant fabrics, often at a lower cost than buying new furniture.

Do you install wooden and PVC flooring for commercial spaces?

Yes. Alongside residential engineered hardwood flooring, we install commercial-grade, waterproof SPC/PVC vinyl flooring suited to high-traffic offices and retail spaces.
